# nba matchups

The NBA Watchability Score is a 0-100 scale that estimates how fun a given NBA game will be to watch. It's not meant to be scientific, just something to consider when deciding whether and what to watch.

The Score takes into account the following factors:

- The two teams' combined net rating (how good the teams are)
- The difference between the two teams' net ratings (how competitive the game will be)
- How much each team is currently weakened by injuries
- Whether one or both of the teams are on a back-to-back (i.e. they played the previous day)

As an NBA nerd, when I'm trying to decide what game(s) to watch, these are mainly the factors I consider. At least, the ones that can be quantified.
